You Can't Optimize a Factory with a Thermometer

Traditional tools—gateways, routers, basic observability—are like a thermometer. They can tell you if your overall spend is "hot," but they have limited visibility into the AI Factory floor. They can't see the misconfigured quantization, the inefficient caching strategy, or the suboptimal model choice for a specific RAG workload.

They weren't built to navigate the 10,000+ interdependent variables of a modern AI factory. To stop the bleed, you need a control plane that can see and optimize the entire, chaotic system.
